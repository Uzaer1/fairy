Poetry for You

There’s something about you I can’t explain,

A warmth in your presence, like sunshine after rain.

Your laughter feels like music to my ears,

And with you, I forget my doubts and fears.


You light up the room with just your smile,

And make the world brighter, even for a while.

I may not say it, but I hope you see,

How much you truly mean to me.


You’re the thought that starts my day,

And the reason my worries drift away.

If I could wish for one thing to be true,

It’d be to spend more moments with you.
